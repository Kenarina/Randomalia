Feel like testing your knowledge about animals?

How Quiz-and-Play works

As the name suggests, this is a two-part game. Part A tests your knowledge of stuff about animals, especially those found in East Africa. In Part B, you play against the Animal Wizard for bragging rights based on your animal class preference.

Ready to dive in? Feel free to select either of the two parts and have fun!
