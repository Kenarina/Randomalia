About Randomalia, the Game

In a fantasy world, the Animal Wizard claims to everything about animals. In this edition of the game, put the wizard's claim to the test, with focus on animals from the Equatorial Savannah Region.

The Game

There are four classes of animals: Mammals, Birds, Fish and Reptiles. Some few animals from these classes form the Predator League.
Play to randomly select an animal. The Animal Wizard will also randomly select an animal.
For either animal choice, a rankscore based on animal class and a survivalscore based in membership to the Predator League will be computed. The player with a higher Total Score wins the round.

To determine the winner of the game, agree on number of moves or number of round wins to aim for.
