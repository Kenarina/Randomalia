Part A

The East African region is rich in wildlife and is a great destination for many nature lovers. Let's see how well you know about wildlife from this region located at the equator

Ready for the Quiz? This is how it works:

  1. Enter your nickname.
  2. Click the "Let's Go!" Button
  3. A question about an animal will be displayed along with a space for your answer.
  4. Write in your answer and click the "Enter" button.
  5. Your answer will be displayed alongside a "Delete" button.
  6. If you want to change your answer, click the "Delete" button. Otherwise, click the "Submit" button.
  7. Your score will be displayed. A correct answer carries a score of 1. An incorrect answer carries a score of 0. Maximum score is 47.
  8. Click the "Next Question" to go to the next question.
  9. A score of 10/47 unlocks Part B by displaying the "Play Part B" button. You can either continue with Part A to improve your score or proceed to Part B.
