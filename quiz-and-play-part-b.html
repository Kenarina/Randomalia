Quiz-and-Play: Part B

Welcome to Part B, "nickname" to the East African Fantasy Kingdom where bragging rights for survival carry the day!

The fact that you are here means that you know a think or two about animals or East Africa or two. That said, how likely are you to survive in the East African Fantasy Animal Kingdom?

Your mission:

Play against the Animal Wizard to win bragging rights.

The Game

In this Fantasy East African Kingdom, four animal classes are ranked 1 to 4 (lowest to highest, respectively) and each class has a class leader.
A few animals collectively representing the four classes belong to the Predator League.

Winning bragging rights will depend on the classes chosen and the animals assigned by the class leaders.

How the game works

  1. Select your favourite animal class.
  2. The class leader randomly assigns you an animal
  3. The Animal Wizard also randomly selects their favourite animal class and an animal is assigned by the respective class leader.
Scoring:
  1. For every player move, two scores are awarded:
    1. Rank score for chosen class (1 for higher rank, 0 for lower rank)
    2. Survival score for animal assigned (1 for Predator League animals, 0 for any other animal)
  2. The player with the highest combined score gets a WIN for the move. Your scores will be displayed.
  3. To win the game, beat your opponent to 11 wins or get most wins over the assigned game time.

That's it! Simple, right?

Along the way, discover the social ranking order of the animal classes.
